STAYFIT HUB
Stayfit – Manufactures & Provide Fitness Solutions for Homes & Commercial Use. Founded on June 24th 1996, STAYFIT has served innumerable customers and helped set up Fitness Centers across India. The company has supplied gym equipments for prestigious clientele from defense sector to elite educational institutions/universities, hotels and resorts to clubs and gyms.
With well over 2 decades of excellence in the fitness industry, stayfit has come up with a stayfit hub, which is an ultimate destination for people to get into healthier lifestyle, with a vision of 100 clubs over the country in next 5 years.
As the complete fitness solution, Stayfit Hub is focused on getting people the best results they want. Top of the line equipments, best in class personal training programs, ideal nutrition counseling, convenient timings and easily accessible facilities provide members the ideal experience and a healthier lifestyle.
